Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1.5 cups
Cocoa powder 0.5 cup
Baking powder 1 tsp
Eggs 3
Milk 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a cake pan.

Step 2: Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.

Step 3: Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.

Source: AllRecipes

Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Ground beef 250g
Tomato sauce 2 cups
Onion 1
Garlic 2 cloves

Step 1: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

Step 2: In a pan, cook ground beef with onions and garlic until browned.

Step 3: Add tomato sauce and simmer for 20 minutes.

Source: BBC Good Food

Caesar Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Romaine lettuce 1 head
Croutons 1 cup
Parmesan cheese 0.5 cup
Caesar dressing 3 tbsp

Step 1: Tear the lettuce into bite-sized pieces and place in a large bowl.

Step 2: Add croutons, cheese, and Caesar dressing. Toss to combine.

Source: Delish
